Help Manual

Welcome to the Voter Authentication System! This manual is designed to help you navigate the application. The system ensures secure voter authentication by combining the use of your Voter ID, Aadhaar Number, and fingerprint verification. It's designed for ease of use, guiding you step by step through the process.

This system is beneficial for ensuring the accuracy and security of your identity. With the rise of digital authentication, this app provides a simple yet highly secure way of verifying voter identities for elections and other important purposes.

Follow the steps below to successfully authenticate your identity using the app.

Steps to Use the Application

  • Enter Your Details: Open the app and enter your valid Voter ID and Aadhaar Number in the respective fields. Double-check that the information is entered correctly before proceeding.
  • Click 'Verify & Proceed': Once you've entered your details, click the 'Verify & Proceed' button. The system will verify the information against the records in the database.
  • Fingerprint Scan: After successful verification, you will be redirected to the fingerprint authentication page. Place your finger on the fingerprint scanner for verification.
  • Successful Authentication: If the fingerprint matches the database record, you will be authenticated successfully, and the app will notify you of your successful verification.
  • If Authentication Fails: If your fingerprint doesn't match, you will be prompted to try again or contact support for assistance.

Features

  • Secure voter authentication using Aadhaar and Voter ID.
  • Fingerprint verification for enhanced security.
  • User-friendly interface for easy navigation.

Troubleshooting

  • If you encounter any issues, make sure the information entered is correct. If the problem persists, feel free to contact support for help with troubleshooting.
