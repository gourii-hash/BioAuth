Information About Major Indian Political Parties

Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP)

Founded: 1980
Symbol: Lotus
Ideology: Nationalism, Hindutva, Economic Reforms
Current Leader: Narendra Modi
✦Strong presence across India especially in North and West regions.
✦BJP is a right-wing party focusing on nationalism, development, and cultural heritage.
✦It promotes Hindutva ideology and policies like "Make in India", "Digital India" & "Sabka Saath, Sabka Vikas".
✦ Major Leaders: Narendra Modi, Amit Shah, Yogi Adityanath.

Indian National Congress (INC)

Founded: 1885
Symbol: Hand
Ideology: Secularism, Social Justice, Inclusive Growth
Current Leader: Mallikarjun Kharge
✦ Played a crucial role in India’s freedom struggle.
✦INC is India 's oldest political party, which led the freedom struggle against British rule.
✦ Follows secularism, social justice, and democratic socialism.
✦ Major Leaders: Rahul Gandhi, Sonia Gandhi, Mallikarjun Kharge.

Aam Aadmi Party (AAP)

Founded: 2012
Symbol: Broom
Ideology: Anti-corruption, Good Governance, Transparency
Current Leader: Arvind Kejriwal
✦ Strong presence in Delhi, Punjab and expanding in other states.
✦Formed after the anti-corruption movement led by Anna Hazare.
✦Focus on transparency, education, healthcare, and free basic facilities. Major Leader: Arvind Kejriwal.

Nationalist Congress Party (NCP)

Founded: 1999
Symbol: Clock
Ideology: Secularism, Democracy, Regional Interests
Current Leader: Sharad Pawar (Faction), Ajit Pawar (Faction)
✦Prominent in Maharashtra and parts of North-East.
✦Focuses on democracy, secularism, and federalism.
✦ Strong presence in Maharashtra.
✦ Major Leaders: Sharad Pawar, Ajit Pawar.

Shiv Sena

Founded: 1966
Symbol: Bow and Arrow / Flaming Torch (factions)
Ideology: Regional Pride, Hindutva, Marathi Identity
Leaders: Eknath Shinde (Shinde faction), Uddhav Thackeray (UBT faction)
✦Strong presence in Maharashtra.
✦Originally promoted Marathi pride & Hindutva ideology.
✦ Now split into Eknath Shinde & Uddhav Thackeray groups.
✦ Current CM of Maharashtra from Shiv Sena (Shinde Faction).
